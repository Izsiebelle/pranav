About Pranav

I grew up in a small town on the seacoast of New Hampshire with my parents and two sisters, as a second-generation Indian-American. After graduating from high school I moved to Washington DC to attend George Washington University. When I started at GW, I thought I had my whole life planned out: I would study International Affairs in our nation's capital, go to Law School, and then move back home to New Hampshire. But, while at GW, I fell in love with the city of DC. Throughout my life, I have been dedicated to working to improve my local community. Whether it was on the soccer field, in the classroom, in different areas around the world, or in the classroom, I have always worked to make a difference in the lives of the people I interact with. And these values are what will lead me in my work if I am elected to represent you as your ANC commissioner.
